Top 3 Games

1. Super Mario Galaxy

Rating: ★★★★★

Super Mario Galaxy is a very popular game from the Super Mario franchize that made its debut in 2007. Mario travels across the galaxy on a quest to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser. There are unique gravity physics and creatures. You can travel from different planets all across the galaxy.

2. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Rating: ★★★★★

The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time was released in 1998 on the Nintendo 64. This game follows a boy named Link who is on a quest to stop Ganondorf from obtaining the Triforce and plunging the land of Hyrule into darkness.

3. Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Rating: ★★★★½

A relaxing life-simulation game where players can build and customize their own island. Known for its charming characters and peaceful gameplay. The game was a hit after its release in 2020 on the Nintendo Switch.
