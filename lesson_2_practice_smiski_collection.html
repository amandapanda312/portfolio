Smiski Collection

What are Smiskis?

Smiskis are popular Japanese, collectible figurines designed to "hide" in corners, shelves, or small spaces. Created by Dreams, these blind-boxed, pale green creatures have neutral, curious faces and are posed engaging in everyday activities like working, yoga, or lounging

They Glow!

Smiskis glow because they're made from a special phosphorescent material (ATBC-PVC) that absorbs light (like sunlight or LED light) and then slowly releases it as a glow in the dark. The brighter and longer they're charged, the better and longer they'll glow, acting like tiny light batteries for your room.
